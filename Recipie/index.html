Recipe for Bread and jams with sunny side up

Ingredients

Fot the bread and jams:

  • 2 piece bread
  • Orange marmalade
  • Mixed fruit
  • Milk half cup

For the sunny side up

  • Two eggs
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2 tsp oil

Instructions

  1. Toast the bread to your preffered amount. I like it a bit crunchy but not too brown
  2. Oil the pan a bit
  3. Add the two eggs to the pan
  4. Fry till you feel satisfied with the value
